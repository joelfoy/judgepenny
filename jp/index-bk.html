The Judge

Experienced.

Judge Penny Brown Reynolds brings years of experience to her many roles. As a distinguished trial court judge and published author, she has lived the life that others can only speak about. The Emmy nominated Executive Producer presides over the syndicated show, “Family Court with Judge Penny.” She is a captivating speaker who specializes in the areas of law, religion and politics, gender and race, women and children’s issues, family relations as well as pop culture and business relations.
